    We have Confidence in our interdisciplinary nature of science and technology

    With our tech-driven scientists and experienced partners, New Life Research Foundation is well positioned to fulfilling its core aims and objectives, which includes innovative interdisciplinary tech-driven researches, counseling & consultancy, scholarship mentorship, and professional training.

    From our Director’s Desk

    Meet our Director, Mr. Joseph Luper Tsenum, as he tells you more about New Life Research Foundation.

    New Life Research Foundation (a Christian nonprofit organization) is the driving force for advancing interdisciplinary tech-driven researches around the world. We are persuaded that God wants to use our academics and research for revival in the nations.

    Over the past years, God has been mobilizing arrows in His quiver in and into different nations through this platform. Through scholarships, research, professional training, and mission work, several of our members have been able to take the ‘Initiation step’ into the many nations that God has been promising them over the years.
