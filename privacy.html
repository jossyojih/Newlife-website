Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting New Life Research Foundation. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our website.

When you visit our website, we may collect the following information about you:

Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number when you fill out a form on our website or contact us through email.
Usage Data:We may also collect information about how you use our website, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, and the pages you view.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:
To provide and maintain our website.
To contact you and respond to your inquiries.
To improve our website and services.
To send you newsletters, promotional materials, and other communications related to our organization, events, and activities.
Disclosure of Your Information
We may disclose your personal information to third parties in the following circumstances:

Service Providers: We may disclose your personal information to our service providers who help us provide our website and services, such as web hosting, email delivery, and payment processing.
Legal Requirements: We may disclose your personal information to comply with applicable laws and regulations or to respond to lawful requests, such as subpoenas or court orders.
Business Transactions: We may disclose your personal information as part of a business transaction, such as a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.
Cookies
We use cookies on our website to improve your user experience. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences and enable certain functions of our website. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but please note that some features of our website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

Security
We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your personal information.
Your Rights You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. You may also object to the processing of your personal information or restrict the use of your personal information in certain circumstances. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided below.

Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated Privacy Policy on our website and indicate the date of the most recent update. Your continued use of our website after we make changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices regarding your personal information, please contact us at newliferesearchfoundation2013@gmail.com.

Code of Conduct

Thank you for being part of New Life Research Foundation. Our Code of Conduct outlines the principles and standards we expect all members, employees, contractors, partners, and stakeholders to uphold in their interactions and conduct within our organization.

Respect and Inclusivity : We conduct our business with honesty, integrity, and transparency. We adhere to high ethical standards and comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and company policies. Conflicts of interest must be disclosed promptly, and we avoid engaging in any activities that may compromise our integrity or reputation.
Integrity and Ethics: We may disclose your personal information to our service providers who help us provide our website and services, such as web hosting, email delivery, and payment processing.
Legal Requirements: We may disclose your personal information to comply with applicable laws and regulations or to respond to lawful requests, such as subpoenas or court orders.
Confidentiality: We recognize the importance of confidentiality in protecting sensitive information belonging to our organization, members, employees, clients, and partners. We maintain the confidentiality of proprietary information and respect the privacy of individuals' personal data in accordance with applicable privacy laws and company policies.
Professionalism:
We strive for excellence in all aspects of our work and interactions. We communicate professionally and courteously with colleagues, members, clients, and stakeholders. We take responsibility for our actions and decisions and seek continuous improvement in our performance.
Workplace:
We are committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment for all employees. We adhere to safety guidelines and procedures to prevent accidents, injuries, and occupational hazards. Employees are encouraged to report any safety concerns or incidents promptly.
Compliance:
We comply with all relevant laws, regulations, and industry standards governing our business activities. We do not engage in or condone any form of bribery, corruption, or illegal behavior. We cooperate fully with regulatory authorities and maintain accurate records and documentation.
Environmental Responsibility:
We are committed to minimizing our environmental impact and promoting sustainable practices. We strive to reduce waste, conserve resources, and minimize pollution in our operations. We support initiatives that contribute to environmental conservation and address climate change.
Social Responsibility:
We actively engage with our communities and strive to make a positive impact through philanthropy, volunteerism, and social initiatives. We support equality under God, both within our organization and in the communities where we operate.
Reporting Violations:
Employees are encouraged to raise concerns or report any violations of this Code of Conduct promptly and through appropriate channels, such as their supervisor, HR department, or designated compliance officer. Reports will be treated confidentially and investigated promptly and impartially.
Consequences and Non-Compliance:
Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment, membership or contractual relationship. We take allegations of misconduct seriously and are committed to enforcing our policies consistently and fairly.

By adhering to this Code of Conduct, we contribute to fostering a positive work environment, upholding our values, and achieving our organizational goals.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices regarding your personal information, please contact us at newliferesearchfoundation2013@gmail.com.
